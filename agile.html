Agile Methods

Agile methods was created by a group of engineers in response to the problems of the waterfall model.

Extreme Pogramming(XP)

XP follows various rules:

  • Project divided into iterations
  • All code must have unit tests
  • Small releases
  • Integrate often
  • Refactor whenever and whenever

Pros of XP:

  • High quality of code
  • Continuous
  • All programmers follow same the rules
  • Sustainable pace - less chance of tired programmers

Cons of XP:

  • Programmers have to be in the same geographic location
  • Customer needs to provide a representative who work with the team on the project
